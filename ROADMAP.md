# Product Roadmap - Phase 1 & 2

## Phase 1: The Intelligent Vault (Q1-Q2)
*   **MVP Launch:** Basic upload with automatic tagging.
*   **Integration:** Direct Figma and Adobe Creative Cloud plugins.
*   **Semantic Search:** Launch natural language search interface.

## Phase 2: The Creative Assistant (Q3-Q4)
*   **Smart Collections:** AI suggests assets that go well together.
*   **Auto-Cleanup:** Identify and archive duplicate or low-quality assets.
*   **Collaboration Hub:** Shared team libraries with automated permission tagging based on project roles.

## Phase 3: Generative Expansion (Next Year)
*   **Asset Variation:** AI-assisted resizing and format conversion.
*   **Contextual Recommendation:** Predict what asset a designer needs based on their current active canvas.
